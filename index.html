StyleList

Have you ever wished that you could save all your favorite clothing items from onilne stores in one place? With StyleList, you can.

StyleList is a consolidated wish list that allows you to keep track of clothing items you're interested in purchasing. As you shop online, you can add your must-have items to your StyleList wish list. Then, through the mobile app, you can view your wish list items, create style boards, sort items by clothing type and share your wish lists with friends and family. The application will also automatically remove items that are out of stock and suggest related clothing items.

How It Works

  1. Download the app on your iOS or Android device, and then start shopping at your favorite online stores.
  2. Save your must-have clothing items to your StyleList wish lists.
  3. Purchase items from your wish lists and share your lists with friends and family.
